Placement & Career Services

We conduct campus placement programs to provide job opportunities to students completing their programs in the current academic year. Top companies from various industries visit our university to recruit talented graduates.

Our dedicated placement cell works year-round to bridge the gap between academia and industry, ensuring our students are well-prepared for the professional world.
