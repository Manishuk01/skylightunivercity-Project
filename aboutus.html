About Skylight University

Discover our rich history, mission, and commitment to academic excellence that has shaped generations of leaders and innovators.

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Skylight University has grown from a small college to a nationally recognized institution of higher learning. Our journey has been marked by innovation, dedication to student success, and a commitment to pushing the boundaries of knowledge.

Today, we stand as a beacon of academic excellence with over 15,000 students across our various programs. Our alumni network spans the globe, making significant contributions in their respective fields.

Our Services & Benefits

At Skylight University, we provide comprehensive support services designed to help students thrive academically, personally, and professionally. From state-of-the-art learning facilities to personalized career counseling, we're committed to your success.

Our benefits include cutting-edge research opportunities, internship programs with industry leaders, study abroad options, and a vibrant campus life that fosters personal growth and lifelong connections.

Our Mission & Vision

We foster our students' love for learning, encourage them to try new and exciting things, and give them a solid foundation to build on. Our vision is to develop well-rounded, confident and responsible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential.

We will do this by providing a welcoming, happy, safe, and supportive learning environment in which everyone is equal and all achievements are celebrated. We believe that a happy student is a successful one.

Career Opportunities

Many jobs require a college degree. Depending on the level of the position you wish to obtain, you may need a certain type of degree. Entry-level positions will often require either an associate's degree or a bachelor's degree.

If you want to specialize in a certain field and work in a senior position, then you may need to consider higher-level degrees, like a master's or doctoral degree. Our career services team is dedicated to helping you navigate these choices.
